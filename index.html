What We Do

Audit, Assessment & Sustainability Services

Specialised auditing, assessment, monitoring and sustainability services to help industries improve resource efficiency and ensure environmental compliance.

Energy Audits

Comprehensive evaluation of energy consumption across electrical, thermal and utility systems to identify savings opportunities and improve efficiency.

Water Audits

Assessment of water usage and distribution patterns to identify losses, improve utilisation and support effective water conservation practices.

Electrical Safety Audits

Systematic inspection of electrical installations to identify safety risks and support compliance with applicable safety standards and regulations.

Thermography & Thermal Imaging

Non-invasive thermal inspections for detecting hotspots, electrical abnormalities and insulation deficiencies that impact safety or performance.

Measurement & Verification

Monitoring and verification of implemented projects to evaluate performance, quantify savings and validate energy conservation measures.

GHG Accounting

Assessment and reporting of greenhouse gas emissions to support carbon footprint management and environmental reporting requirements.

Energy Performance Assessment

Detailed performance evaluation of utility systems, HVAC, cooling towers, pumps, blowers and motors to identify improvement opportunities.

Sustainability Consulting

Consultancy focused on energy efficiency, water conservation, carbon reduction and sustainable operational practices for long-term growth.
